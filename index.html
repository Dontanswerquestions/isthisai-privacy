Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Introduction

IsthisAI? ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

1. Video Content

  • What we collect: Videos you upload or analyze through our App
  • How we use it: To perform AI analysis, detect whether videos are AI-generated, and generate video summaries
  • Storage: Videos are temporarily stored on our servers for analysis purposes and are automatically deleted after processing (typically within 24 hours)
  • Transcription: Audio from videos may be transcribed to generate summaries; transcripts are processed but not permanently stored

2. Device Information

  • What we collect: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers
  • How we use it: To ensure compatibility, improve app performance, and prevent fraud

3. Usage Data

  • What we collect: Number of analyses performed, features used, subscription status, purchase history
  • How we use it: To improve our services, understand user needs, and provide personalized experiences

4. Payment Information

  • What we collect: Subscription status, purchase history, transaction IDs (processed through Google Play/App Store)
  • How we use it: To manage subscriptions, process payments, and provide customer support
  • Storage: Payment information is handled by Google Play/App Store; we do not store credit card details

Permissions Required

  • Media Library Access: Required to select videos from your device for analysis
  • Internet Access: Required to upload videos to our servers for analysis
  • Network State: Required to check connectivity before uploading videos

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI video analysis services and video summarization
  • Improve our detection algorithms and AI models
  • Ensure app functionality and performance
  • Process subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Respond to user feedback and support requests
  • Send important service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

  • Video Storage: Videos are stored temporarily on secure servers during analysis and are automatically deleted after processing (typically within 24 hours)
  • Local Storage: Some data (like analysis history, subscription status) is stored locally on your device
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure servers) to protect your data

Third-Party Services

  • Google AI Studio (Gemini): For advanced video analysis and summarization - Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play / App Store: For subscription and payment processing
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to free users - AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Railway: Backend hosting - Railway Privacy Policy
  • Expo: App platform - Expo Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share videos with third parties except as necessary for analysis
  • Use your videos for training our AI models without explicit consent
  • Store videos permanently
  • Track you across other websites or apps

Advertising

Free users may see advertisements via Google AdMob. We do not share personal video data with advertisers. Pro subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the app and delete local data
  • Subscription Cancellation: Cancel your Pro subscription at any time through app store settings

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
